Case Study: Luna & Sol Organics

Shopify • Skincare • USA
Client

Eco‑friendly skincare brand selling via Shopify: facial oils, moisturizers, and soaps.

Problem

High sales volume with limited visibility into profitability by SKU and channel. Manual reporting.

Objective

Establish an operating model with P&L by product/channel and a reinvestment plan tied to ROI.

Key Questions
  • Which products generate the highest and lowest margins?
  • Which channels contribute most to revenue and profit?
  • How do discounts and shipping policies impact margin?
  • Which SKUs should be phased out or doubled down on?
  • What is the optimal allocation of marketing spend?
  • Where can automation/AI reduce manual effort or improve decisions?
Methodology
  1. Scope and align on goals, constraints, and KPIs.
  2. Structure data to consistently answer the key questions.
  3. Build a lightweight P&L by product and by channel.
  4. Design a founders’ dashboard and review cadence.
  5. Recommend actions and a reinvestment plan.
Key Findings
  • Top product: Rosehip Facial Oil – 45% margin.
  • Top channel: Instagram Checkout, +30% AOV vs. others.
  • Underperformer: Aloe & Mint Soap – 8% margin due to high COGS and discounting.
  • Cost leak: Free shipping lowered margin ~12% on low‑ticket orders.
